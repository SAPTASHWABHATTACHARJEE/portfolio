Academic History
  • 2005
    I started going to school for the first time. I was admitted to St. Mary's School, Malda, West Bengal in 2005. I studied there from Toddler to Class 3. This school is affiliated to the Indian Council of Secondary Education (ICSE).
  • 2012
    My family had to shift from Malda to Kolkata in 2012 owning to my father's transferrable job. In 2012, I took admission in B.D.M.International, Kolkata, West Bengal. I studied here from class 4 to class 12 (high school graduation). B.D.M.I. is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
  • 2019
    I secured 97% in the CBSE class 10 board examination, a.k.a. AISCE.
  • 2021
    1. I secured 97.4% in the CBSE class 12 board examination, a.k.a. AISSCE.
    2. I secured an All India Rank of 620 in the Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY) SX category, organized by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore. Thereby, I became a KVPY Fellow.
    3. I secured an All India Rank of 864 in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Mains.
    4. I secured a General Merit Rank of 8 in the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE) and was felicitated by Mamata Banerjee, Chief Minister of West Bengal.
    5. I secured an All India Rank of 1081 in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced and secured admission to the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
  • 2022
    I received the Jagadis Bose National Science Talent Search (JBNSTS) Senior Scholarship, awarded by the Government of West Bengal. I am now a JBNSTS Senior Scholar.
  • 2024
    1. I was honoured as an Undergraduate Research Fellow by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) for the tapeout of a Radiation-Hardened SERDES IC in 180nm SCL.
    2. I have been awarded the DAAD WISE Scholarship to carry out a fully funded research internship at TU Munich in the summer of 2024. I am now a DAAD Fellow.
    3. I have secured the Mitacs Globalink Research Internship at the École de Technologie Supérieure (ETS) Montréal in the summer of 2024.
  • 2025
    Anticipated completion of Bachelors of Technology in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
